How To Play

  • Home
  • How To Play

1. Create Account / Log In

  • Click Login / Sign Up on the website.
  • Sign up with your email ID + password
  • If you already have an account, simply log in.

2. Get Ready on Your PC

  • Install Steam (if not installed).
  • Sign up or log in to your Steam account.
  • Download and install CS2 (Counter-Strike 2) from Steam.

3. Click Play CS2

  • After login, on the homepage, click:
  • Play CS2 (₹1 for 1 Hour)
  • This takes you to the Match Details Page.

4. Pay ₹1 to Join Match

  • On the match page, click:
  • Play Now ₹1 per Hour
  • Make payment securely or deduct for your Wallet.

5. Get Your Server Code

  • After successful payment, you’ll see:
  • Your Server Code: [XXX XXX]
  • Click Copy Server Code and then Go to Server.

6. Join the Match

  • Launch CS2 on your PC.
  • Open the CS2 console (press the ~ key).
  • Type or paste the following command:
  • connect [Server IP]; password [Your Server Code]
  • Hit Enter to join the server.

7. Play & Enjoy!

  • Your 1-hour session starts once you enter the server.
  • Enjoy smooth and fair gameplay on our servers!
